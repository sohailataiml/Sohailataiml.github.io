Why Choose Dr. Ali Plastic Surgery

Dr. Ali Plastic Surgery & AMAE Med Spa is the Detroit area’s one stop for the widest array of cosmetic surgeries and state-of-the-art, non-invasive aesthetic treatments. Our highly qualified staff offers the most advanced surgical procedures such as breast enhancement, tummy tucks, liposuction, facelifts, rhinoplasty (nose jobs), mommy makeovers, and more.

We also offer the latest non-surgical fat loss, skin tightening, and anti aging treatments, including CoolSculpting, Botox, Juvderm, Microneedling, and Profound & C02RE laser skin rejuvenation.

Dr. Ali is one of the Detroit area’s top plastic surgeons. It is his belief that patient education and realistic expectation are the key to the success of any plastic surgery procedure. He will take the time to get to know you, listen to your appearance concerns, understand your aesthetic goals, and provide you with the information and options to make the best decisions regarding the procedures that are right for you.

Meet Top Birmingham Plastic Surgeon Dr. M. Azhar Ali, MD, FACS

Dr. Ali is board certified in plastic surgery, a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and a Fellow in the American College of Surgeons. He has performed more than 7,000 cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries.

He has been awarded Top Doc by Hour Detroit Magazine multiple years in a row. He is also a RealSelf Top Doc, and has a perfect 5 star rating from his patients on Yelp!, Google and Facebook. What sets the best plastic surgeons like Dr. Ali apart is extensive training and years of experience – combined with a passion for improving patients’ lives by enhancing their appearance.

Dr. Ali has performed thousands of breast enhancements, breast lifts, tummy tucks, liposuctions, facelifts, nose jobs, arm lifts, mommy makeovers, and breast reconstructions, using the latest surgical techniques and the highest possible standards of safety and care. In addition to being one of Birmingham’s top plastic surgeons, Dr. Ali also offers a wide array of the most advanced laser and radiofrequency skin rejuvenating and anti-aging treatments.

Dr. Ali also takes time from his busy schedule every year to travel worldwide on humanitarian medical missions. He annually volunteers his surgical services in underprivileged areas across the globe to give back smiles to children.
